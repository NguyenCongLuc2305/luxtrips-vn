Popular Destinations

Known as Vietnam's Saigon City, immerse yourself in the rich culture of Ho Chi Minh City! This Vietnamese city is a trove of historical sights waiting to be discovered. Spend half a day at the Cu Chi Tunnels and learn about its significance during the Viet Cong war. Travel like a local and hop...

on a motorbike tour through the bustling streets, exploring hidden alleyways and sampling authentic street food. Visit the iconic Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica of Saigon and the Central Post Office, architectural marvels from the French colonial era.

Don't miss the War Remnants Museum for a sobering look at Vietnam's history, and explore the vibrant Ben Thanh Market for souvenirs and local delicacies. Experience the city's modern side at the Bitexco Financial Tower's Saigon Skydeck for panoramic views.

Things to Note When Traveling to Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City consists of multiple urban and suburban districts, including central districts and outlying areas.

As of 2021, the population of Ho Chi Minh City has exceeded 9 million people, making it one of the most vibrant and bustling metropolitan areas in Vietnam.

The city is famous for its vibrant street life, rich history, diverse cuisine, and dynamic economic activity.

Visitors should try local specialties such as pho, banh mi, broken rice (com tam), and a wide variety of street food.

The best time to visit Ho Chi Minh City is during the dry season, from December to April, when the weather is more pleasant.
