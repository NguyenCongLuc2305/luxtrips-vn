Itinerary
DAY 1
HANOI GET SETTLED

Welcome to Vietnam! Please note: Your travel documents contain a Visa-on-Arrival letter (unless otherwise arranged). Please ensure to pack this in your carry-on luggage. Your Visa-on-Arrival letter must be presented to Customs upon entry into the country.

Upon arrival to Hanoi's Noi Bai International Airport you will be welcomed by a hostess holding a sign with your name on it. The hostess will guide you through fast-track security services with your pre-approved E-Visa before escorting you to collect your luggage and then onwards to your awaiting guide and vehicle.

Meet your private driver and English-speaking guide for an airport transfer.

DAY 2
Where you’ll be staying

Embark on a poignant journey today as you explore the history of Hoa Lo Prison, once infamous among American soldiers as the "Hanoi Hilton." Led by a knowledgeable local guide and joined by Mr. Chuck Searcy, a former U.S. Army member turned Vietnam Veterans' Memorial Fund project manager, this guided tour offers a unique perspective of the gruesome wartime period. Explore the remaining fragments of the prison, which served as both a French colonial-era penitentiary and a symbol of resilience during the Vietnam War. Gain insights into the past while supporting efforts to heal the wounds of conflict in the region. Delve into the heart of Project RENEW, an organization dedicated to eradicating the peril of unexploded munitions in Central Vietnam. As co-chair of the Agent Orange Working Group, Mr. Searcy's insights are invaluable. With a rich history, from U.S. Army service to intelligence analysis during the war, his perspective is unparalleled.

This evening, come hungry as you embark on a culinary journey through Hanoi's Old Quarter with your private guide. This evening, you will be treated to an array of tantalizing Vietnamese specialties that define the city's world famous food scene. Delight in the soft and savory banh cuon, followed by the iconic banh mi sandwich, renowned even in the Oxford Dictionary. Quench your thirst at a Bia Hoi on Ta Hien Street, savoring the freshness of Vietnamese beer beloved by locals and travelers alike. Next, visit a pho shop to relish the classic pho or try the unique pho tron, mixing pho noodles with chicken. Conclude your foodie evening with a stroll along Hoan Kiem Lake, enjoying a delicious ice cream before being returned to your accommodation, having experienced the vibrant flavors of Hanoi.

Where you’ll be staying
  • Sofitel Legend Metropole Hanoi
  • Opera Wing, Premium Room
DAY 3
Hanoi A Healthy Dose of History
Where you’ll be staying

Journey through history on a half-day tour with a private guide, discovering the iconic landmarks of Hanoi. Begin at Ba Dinh Square, where you will admire the imposing facade of the iconic Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, the revered resting site of revolutionary leader Ho Chi Minh. Explore the city's traditional stilt houses, showcasing a unique architectural style, allowing for ventilation and protection from flooding in the city's historic neighborhoods. Next, admire the architecture of the One-Pillar Pagoda, originally built in 1049, and known for it's distinctive lotus-shape, the pagoda stands as a symbol of devotion and resilience. Continue your exploration at the thousand-year-old Temple of Literature, the first university in Vietnam. Originally built in 1070, it served as an exclusive institution for princes, nobility, and mandarins. Stroll through the shaded paths of Van Mieu Park, marveling at the historic architecture and discovering 82 of the original 116 engraved steles that bear the names of past graduates. Immerse yourself in the rich history and cultural significance of these iconic sites, gaining a deeper understanding of Hanoi's past, before you are transferred back to your accommodation.

Step into the theater and witness an ancient art form that traces its roots back over a thousand years to the farmers of the Red River Delta. Originally a peasant hobby, water puppetry gained royal status under the Ly and Tran dynasties. Immerse yourself in a mesmerizing performance that depicts scenes from daily life and legendary tales. The puppeteers skillfully maneuver the puppets on the water, accompanied by traditional music that adds depth and emotion to the storytelling. Delight in this unique form of entertainment that showcases the rich cultural heritage of Vietnam. At the end of the show, you will be transferred back to your accommodation.

DAY 4
Hanoi City Streets and Tasty Treats
Where you’ll be staying

This morning, enjoy time at leisure to explore at your own pace, or relax at your hotel.

It's time for an exhilarating private guided journey through the hidden gems of Hanoi aboard a vintage Soviet-era jeep. Departing from your hotel, venture into lesser-known corners of the city, meandering through maze-like alleyways and encountering the authentic essence of Hanoi. Engage with locals along the way and embrace the diverse and sometimes eccentric aspects of the city. Make a memorable stop at one of Hanoi's many bustling markets, then visit a smaller, local market in a remote area to immerse yourself in the vibrant bustling atmosphere. Continue the adventure, passing by the must-see highlights of the capital before reaching the scenic West Lake, where you'll savor local culinary delights. Return to your starting point, capturing lasting memories with photographs of this unique experience. Throughout the tour, indulge in traditional Vietnamese dishes, from finger foods to the renowned egg coffee and other delightful traditional beverages.

  • Sofitel Legend Metropole Hanoi
  • Opera Wing, Premium Room
DAY 5
Hanoi to Halong Bay All Aboard!
Where you’ll be staying

Meet your private driver and English-speaking guide for a transfer to Halong Bay. Approximate travel time is 3 hours.

Welcome aboard!

Take some time to get settled in to your stateroom, and to explore the length of your ship. You'll be welcomed aboard by the captain and crew with a Welcome Drink in the on-board restaurant, as you are briefed on the next two days of your cruise.

Set out on an adventure to Luon Cave, a hidden haven ideal for exploring by kayak. For a more serene voyage, you may opt to paddle the lagoon in a bamboo boat. No matter which vessel you choose, you will have an opportunity to get up close and personal with Halong Bay's iconic limestone karsts.

Journey to Titop Island, known for it's iconic crescent moon-shaped beach and tranquil blue waters. You may choose to relax on the beach, enjoy a refreshing swim, or climb the 400 steps to the island's look-out point, boasting panoramic views of Halong Bay from above.

As the sun begins to set, admire the picturesque views from the Sky Bar, while you opt to enjoy a drink with your fellow cruise mates at Happy Hour. Afterward, join a cooking demonstration guided by the onboard chef, where you will learn how to make traditional Vietnamese dishes.

Relax with your fellow cruise mates in the poker club, enjoy a night-cap at one of the two on-board bars, experience a squid fishing demonstration or simply unwind on your balcony and watch as night descends upon Halong Bay.

Sofitel Legend Metropole Hanoi

Located steps from the Opera House in Hanoi’s iconic French Quarter, the Sofitel Legend Metropole Hanoi welcomes guests with an air of colonial grandeur and timeless elegance. Originally crafted by French aristocrats in the early 1900’s, the property combines old-world charm and history with modern convenience and luxury. Offering 364 rooms and suites across two wings, guests can choose from the historical grandeur of the Metropole Wing or the neo-classical elegance of the Opera Wing. All rooms are equipped with Sofitel’s MyBed, in-room Wi Fi, safety deposit box and minibar. Well-appointed dining options range from Le Beaulieu, serving fine French cuisine, to Spices Garden for local Hanoi specialties. Tucked away in a private oasis on the grounds, the hotel’s stately swimming pool offers carefree relaxation in sophisticated surroundings adjacent to a fitness center equipped with state of the art equipment.
